Read the Instructions before you proceed

Make sure you have a valid mobile number and email address to login to the Online BO Opening System
Make sure you have a NID/Smart Card (Only 10 digit and 17 digit NID is accepted)
If you have the old 13 digit NID, you should add your birthyear at the front of your NID to make it 17 digit. For example, if your 13 digit NID is 1917431000040 and your birth year (as per NID) is 1982, then your 17 digit NID is 19821917431000040
Make sure you have a scanned Passport copy for NRB proof
Make sure you have a Checkbook/Bank certificate copy of your Bank account to find out 9 digit routing number and 13 digit bank a/c number
Make sure you have a digital scanned Photo and signature
Set camera resolution below 5 megapixels
Scanned/ camera-captured files must be in image format (jpg, png, etc.) and size less than 1 MB
Check if your NID information is up to date, as we will fetch your basic info from NID during the process
Mobile Financial System (MFS) account (i.e. bkash, nagad, etc) or VISA/Mastercard or internet banking is required for making payment
